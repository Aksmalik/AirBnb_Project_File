🏡 Airbnb Market Analysis

A dynamic Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb listings across major global cities, focusing on pricing patterns, seasonal booking trends, accommodation types, and customer ratings. The project enables users to explore market demand, identify popular price segments, and evaluate guest satisfaction across different locations.
________________________________________
  Short Description / Purpose
  
The Airbnb Market Analysis Dashboard provides an interactive overview of Airbnb performance metrics including listings, reviews, pricing ranges, seasonal demand, and rating categories across multiple global cities.

This dashboard is designed for hospitality analysts, tourism strategists, property investors, and short-term rental hosts who want to understand market demand, pricing opportunities, and customer satisfaction trends in the Airbnb ecosystem.
________________________________________
  Tech Stack
  
The dashboard was developed using the following technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop

 Primary platform used to design the interactive dashboard and data visualizations.
📂 Power Query

 Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation, ensuring the dataset was structured properly for analysis.
 
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Implemented for calculated measures, KPIs, conditional logic, and dynamic filtering.

🧩 Data Modeling

Relationships between datasets such as listings, reviews, pricing ranges, and seasonal trends were created to enable cross-filtering and meaningful aggregations.

  Features / Highlights
  
• Business Problem


The short-term rental market has grown rapidly worldwide, but analyzing market demand, pricing strategies, and guest satisfaction across cities can be difficult without structured insights.

Key questions include:

•	Which price ranges attract the most bookings?

•	What types of accommodations are most popular?

•	Which cities have the highest customer ratings?

•	How does seasonality affect bookings?

•	Where are high-value pricing opportunities in the market?

Without a visual analytics tool, answering these questions requires complex data analysis.

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

1️⃣ Market Overview (Key KPI Section)

This section provides a high-level snapshot of Airbnb market performance.

Key KPIs include:

•	Total Listings: 279,541

•	Total Reviews: 5,373,143

•	Average Price: $608.57

•	Average Review Score: 93.41

These indicators provide a quick overview of market size, engagement, and overall customer satisfaction.
________________________________________
2️⃣ Booking Distribution by Price Range

A bar chart visualizes how bookings are distributed across different pricing segments.
Key insights:

•	$0–100 price range: 121K bookings (highest demand)

•	$100–200: 58K bookings

•	$200–300: 26K bookings

•	$300–400: 16K bookings

•	Premium listings ($2000+): 14K bookings

•	Lowest demand: $900–1000 range with only 5K bookings

💡 Insight:

Most Airbnb users prefer budget-friendly options, while luxury listings form a smaller but stable niche market.
________________________________________
3️⃣ Accommodation Type Distribution

A donut chart shows the distribution of bookings by property type.
Breakdown:

•	Entire Place: 65% of bookings

•	Private Room: 31% of bookings

•	Hotel Room & Shared Room: Less than 3% combined

💡 Insight:

Guests strongly prefer entire homes or apartments, indicating demand for privacy and independent stays.
________________________________________
4️⃣ City Rating Comparison

A bar chart ranking cities by average rating score highlights differences in customer satisfaction.

Top-rated cities:

•	Mexico City — 94.84

•	Rio de Janeiro — 94.58

•	Cape Town — 94.40

Mid-range ratings:

•	New York

•	Rome

•	Sydney

•	Paris

•	Bangkok

Lowest in the chart:

•	Istanbul — 91.06

•	Hong Kong — 89.73

💡 Insight:

Despite similar service models, guest satisfaction varies significantly across cities, likely due to differences in host quality, property standards, and tourism infrastructure.
________________________________________
5️⃣ Seasonal Booking Analysis

This section visualizes booking trends across cities and seasons.
Key observations:

•	Highest booking activity occurs at the beginning of the year

•	Bookings decline gradually during the following months

•	A mid-year increase occurs around June–July

•	Demand drops again towards the end of the year

Example booking volumes:

•	Paris — 65K

•	New York — 37K

•	Sydney — 34K

•	Rome — 28K

•	Hong Kong — 7K

💡 Insight:

Booking patterns reflect global travel seasons, holidays, and summer tourism peaks.
